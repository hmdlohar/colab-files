## Design Context

### Users
This is an internal media-workflow workspace for a technically comfortable operator who uploads audio and video, waits for jobs to finish, and downloads transcripts or edited media. The job is task completion, not browsing.

### Brand Personality
Practical, calm, precise.

### Aesthetic Direction
Industrial utilitarian dashboard with a persistent side menu, dense but readable forms, and a warm neutral palette with a restrained green accent. It should feel like a working tool hub that can grow over time, not a marketing site.

### Design Principles
- Keep navigation stable and make all tools reachable from a sidebar.
- Show outputs, download links, and job status without hiding them behind modals.
- Reveal advanced options progressively, not all at once.
- Use clear hierarchy and compact spacing so the interface scans quickly.
- Make future tools easy to add without changing the overall layout.
